SonnyLabs PII Checker

How do I make sure I am not about to send Personally Identifiable information to users?

It's a really bad idea for your website (or chatbot) to start giving out names and addresses. Sonny Labs has a tool that will scan your text and highlight any Personally Identifiable information.

You can sign up for a free account at sonnylabs.com. They give you an API key and an analysis ID. You can test it below.

The whole PII checker is just a few lines of code. SonnyLabs helps people make sure LLMs are not being sent or returning information they should not.
